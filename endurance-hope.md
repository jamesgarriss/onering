# Endurance and Hope

Endurance and Hope are the fundamental resources that keep a character going. 

## Endurance

* Bardings = 22 + Heart
* Beornings = 24 + Heart
* Dwarves = 28 + Heart
* Elves = 22 + Heart
* Hobbits = 16 + Heart
* Woodmen = 20 + Heart

## Hope

* Bardings = 8 + Heart 
* Beornings = 8 + Heart 
* Dwarves = 6 + Heart 
* Elves = 8 + Heart 
* Hobbits = 12 + Heart 
* Woodmen = 10 + Heart
